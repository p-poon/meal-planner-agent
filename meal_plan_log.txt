        - Pour about 1/4 cup batter per pancake.

        - For Claypot Tofu: Heat oil in a wok/claypot, brown minced pork.
        - Add carrots and mushrooms, stir-fry.
        - Add tofu, oyster sauce, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, white pepper, and chicken stock.
        - Bring to a simmer.
        - Thicken with cornstarch slurry.
        - For Steamed Eggs: Whisk eggs with stock/water, a pinch of salt.
        - Strain mixture.
        - Gently pour into a heatproof dish.
        - Place marinated minced pork on top.
        - Steam for 10-12 minutes until set but still wobbly.
        - Drizzle with sesame oil and garnish with spring onion.
        - Serve all with steamed rice.
